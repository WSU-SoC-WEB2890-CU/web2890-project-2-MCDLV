About

Michelle will soon be hanging up her wings in the aviation industry as a corporate flight attendant (HR & AP). Falcons and Gulfstreams were her 'offices in the sky' but, she is positioned to pursue a different kind of passion.

Since the lockdown, Michelle has been dabbling in acrylics using brushes, knives, chopsticks, and fingers. Experimenting within the shield of her tiny Texas townhome, she has discovered her euphoric space.

She has an intense thirst for creating (whether modest or taboo), which yields a joyous experience and, her pieces are evidence of that.

-Michelle Angela De La Vega
